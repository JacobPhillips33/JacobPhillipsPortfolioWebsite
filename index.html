bio

I am a Petroleum Engineer turned Full-Stack Software Engineer who specializes in creating responsive web apps using a variety of languages and frameworks including ASP.NET MVC, C#, HTML, JS, CSS, and SQL. I enjoy taking complex problems and breaking them down into manageable parts using my problem-solving abilities. I am highly disciplined, detailed oriented, and very determined to solve any problem. I am also wired to create systems to help projects and people run more efficiently.

I love all aspects of the coding world and am so excited to keep learning and growing. When I'm not at my computer you can find me hiking with my wife and dog, golfing, or learning more about coding (I'm slightly obsessed).
